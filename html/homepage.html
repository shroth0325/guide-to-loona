by Shroth

A Brief Overview of the Group

  • LOONA is a South-Korean girl group formed by Blockberry Creative.
  • LOONA debuted on August 19th, 2018 at the LOONAbirth concert, but released their debut EP, [+ +], a day later with the lead single "Hi High".
  • They are known for their unique pre-debut project in which each member is revealed with their own single album. They also have a storyline, often referred to as the LOONAverse in their music videos.
  • They have three sub-units, LOONA 1/3, LOONA Odd Eye Circle, and LOONA yyxy
  • Each member has a representative color and animal, some members also have representative fruits.
