A Brief Overview of the Group

LOONA is a twelve-membered South-Korean girl group formed by Blockberry Creative in 2018. They debuted on August 19, 2018 at the LOONAbirth concert and released their debut EP, [+ +] the next day with the title track "Hi High". They are mainly known for their pre-debut project in which each member is revealed with a single album. Each member has a representative color and animal and some members have fruits as well. The group also has three sub-units, LOONA 1/3, LOONA Odd Eye Circle, LOONA yyxy.
